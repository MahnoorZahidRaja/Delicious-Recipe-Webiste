  1. 200g Spaghetti
  2. 150g Ground Beef
  3. 1 Onion, chopped
  4. 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  5. 1 can Tomato Sauce
  6. Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook onions and garlic until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
