Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Flour
  2. 1 cup Butter
  3. 1 cup Brown Sugar
  4. 1 tsp Baking Soda
  5. 2 cups Chocolate Chips
  6. 1 Egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Mix in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add flour, baking soda, and chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes.
